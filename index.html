What is the FFO calculator?
The FFO calculator is a tool that measures the funds from the operation of a company.
How to calculate FFO?
To calculate FFO you should use the following formula:
FFO = net income + depreciation and amortization + losses - gains - interest income
Why use FFO calculator?
The FFO calculator is used in order to determine the funds from the operation of a company.
